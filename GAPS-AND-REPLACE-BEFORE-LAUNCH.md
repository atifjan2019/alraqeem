## 1. Combined gaps report, real data not invented

Per the no fabrication rule, the following are not on the page as facts, and
route to inquiry or to a motif panel instead. Send the real values and they
fold in.

1. **Named hotels.** Every package quotes the hotel at inquiry rather than
   naming it, since the exact hotel shifts by date and availability. The audits
   listed real options (for example Serena Swat, Hunza Elites, the Eagle's Nest
   hotel, Shigar Fort and Khaplu Palace as heritage stays, cave hotels in
   Cappadocia), but none are stated as a booked inclusion. Send the hotels you
   actually use per package and per tier, and we name them.

2. **Exact day by day plans.** Itineraries read as the typical flow, confirmed
   for each departure. Exact per departure day plans stay at booking.

3. **Your own photos.** The card photos are representative images, openly
   licensed from Wikimedia Commons and credited on /photo-credits. Your own trip
   photos are stronger, especially on the domestic pages where they are the
   authenticity edge. Priority swaps:
   - **Baku, the Gabala slots.** Three slots stay on the branded panel, no free
     Commons photo of Gabala or the Tufandag cable car was available. A real
     photo removes the last panels on the site.
   - **Same place reuses**, where a nearby real photo stands in for a spot with
     no free image of its own: Swat Ushu Forest shows the Kalam pine forest,
     Kumrat Kala Chashma and Thal show the Kumrat forest and the Panjkora,
     Chitral Shahi Masjid shows the fort beside it, Hunza Eagle's Nest shows
     Rakaposhi, Kalash Chitral town shows the fort, Naran town shows the Kunhar.
   - **Hero images.** The page heroes are not yet your own photography.

4. **Real reviews.** The reviews block runs on staging placeholders, and review
   schema stays off until real reviews are connected.

5. **Accreditation and licence number.** IATA, DTS, or association numbers are
   placeholders, no accreditation schema while placeholder.

6. **Years in operation.** No specific year count is stated, since it is not
   confirmed. Send the founding year and we state it.

7. **Consultant or agent name.** The named consultant is a staging placeholder.

8. **Currency conversion rates.** Two grids carry an approximate rate, the Baku
   manat at about 165 PKR and the Singapore dollar at about 216 PKR, at your
   request. These drift with the exchange rate, so refresh them periodically or
   say the word and we move to a qualitative note.
